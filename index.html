Firmware Updates Available


The UI/UX team was given the task of a redesign of the Firmware Updates Available page and flow. We began by analyzing the flow of completing a simple task of updating a firmware by creating a campaign. Once we created a flowchart we were able to have an overview understanding that the flow was not the main concern, but rather the information architecture and UI. We found that simple tasks such as creating a campaign are made to be complex and take you through an confusing journey that gives little to no context of what the end goal is.

Hueristics Report


Visibility of System Status
  • Visual representation of upgrading and downgrading has proven to be confusing Status of a campaign is represented by color. Users do not know at a glance what the colors represent
  • Uploaded firmware images are files with cumbersome long names. No meta information is provided. Without meta info providing context it is unclear what an uploaded firmware image corresponds with
  • It's unclear which recipe a campaign is referring to
  • Difficult to understand at a glance which updates are scheduled or in progress withoutswitching to a different tile
  • When an Automated Campaign is created, the main information related to the recipe is not being shown
Recognition Rather than Recall
  • Too many clicks required, People get lost in the workflow
  • When the Campaign is completed or Cancelled, it shows them in random order. User wants to see it in chronological order
  • in updates available user wants to see the recipe associated with the update and be able to dig deep
Flexibility and efficiency of use:
  • If a device model has more than 1 OUI, they will have to update each OUI separately
  • Firmware update flow is complicated with many steps
Match between system and real world:
  • no representation of completion % of in progress campaigns
  • false positive reports are confusing customer and they are calling support
User control and freedom:
  • User wants to be able to exclude MAC's when exporting the CSV Reporting document
  • Ability to dig deep into a CMTS and see all of the device models in it
Consistency and standards:
  • When the Campaign is completed or Cancelled, it shows them in random order. User wants to see it in chronological order
